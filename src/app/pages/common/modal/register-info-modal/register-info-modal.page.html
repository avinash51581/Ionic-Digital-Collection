Registration Guidelines!

  • Full Name : Enter your firstname,middlename and lastname.
  • Email : Enter any email address to register in the app,it can be different than your gmail account.
  • Mobile No : Enter your 10 digits mobile number,do not enter country code ex:+91 or 0.
  • Date of Birth : Enter you birth date (date,month,year).
  • Password : Enter password, it must be at least 6 characters long.
